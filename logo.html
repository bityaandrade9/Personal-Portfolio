After hearing this she was eager for me to make a logo for her start up business. At first I was trying to decline because I had only understood the basics of digital designing but she insisted and encouraged me. So I took on the task, she had provided me with the original drawing of what she wanted me to design, plus the colors, font etc. so that saved me plenty of brainstorming.
